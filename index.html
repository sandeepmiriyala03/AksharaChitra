  • 💾 Save, Download, or Share posters directly
  • 🌈 Customize fonts, colors, glow/shadow effects & layout
  • ✨ Comes with built-in templates for birthdays, quotes, invites & more
  • 🔒 Your data is never uploaded — all creations stay safely in your own browser
  • ♿ Fully accessible — compliant with ADA & W3C accessibility standards

📘 Help & Tips

Welcome to the AksharaChitra Help Center! Here’s a quick guide to help you design beautiful posters effortlessly — even offline.

🌈 Getting Started

AksharaChitra is designed for everyone — from beginners to creative professionals. Follow these simple steps to start creating your own poster:

  1. 🖋️ Set your text: Type your title, subtitle, and message. Adjust fonts and alignment as needed.
  2. 🖼️ Add an image: Upload and crop your photo. Choose its position — top, bottom, or background.
  3. 🎨 Style your design: Pick font colors, background tones, and glow or shadow effects for contrast.
  4. 💾 Generate and save: View your live preview, save your work, download, or share instantly.
  5. 📲 Use offline: Install AksharaChitra as an app (PWA) and create posters anytime — even without Internet!

💡 Pro Tips

  • ✨ Use short, catchy titles — they make your design stand out.
  • 📏 Keep font sizes balanced between title and message for visual harmony.
  • 🎯 Dark text looks great on light backgrounds (and vice versa) for perfect contrast.
  • 🔤 Explore different font families for cultural or festive designs.
  • 🌐 Try multiple languages — AksharaChitra supports Indian regional scripts beautifully.

♿ Accessibility

AksharaChitra fully supports WCAG 2.2 and ADA standards to ensure an inclusive experience.

  • 🔈 Screen reader friendly — every button and label is properly announced.
  • ⌨️ Keyboard accessible — navigate all controls using Tab and Enter.
  • 🌗 Theme aware — optimized for both light and dark modes.
  • 🧭 Logical reading order — structured for clarity and usability.
